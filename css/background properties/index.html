madara uchiha

Madara Uchiha is a fictional character and one of the main antagonists in the manga and anime series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. He was the legendary leader of the Uchiha clan and a co-founder of the Hidden Leaf Village (Konohagakure), alongside his rival and friend, Hashirama Senju.
